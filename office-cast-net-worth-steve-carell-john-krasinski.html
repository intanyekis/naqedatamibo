Here's The Cast Of The Office, Ranked By Net Worth

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  11 minutes  |  2342 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Highlights

  • The Office stars have seen a significant increase in their net worth since the show ended, with some earning millions of dollars.
  • Angela Kinsey, known for her role as Angela Martin, has a net worth of $8 million and has continued to work on other projects.
  • The lovable secretary Pam Beesly, played by Jenna Fischer, has a net worth of $12 million and has appeared in other comedies alongside The Office.

The ninth and final season of The Office ended in 2013, but fans would continue to stream the show on Netflix for years, making the comedy one of the most-watched shows on the streaming platform. It would later move to Peacock, the streaming service for NBCUniversal - and once again, fans just kept binging the sensation.

It's safe to say, The Office is a beloved piece of programming, and that is certainly reflected in its stars' bank accounts.

Over the years, most of the main cast members from The Office have continued to work and grow their net worths since the show. Steve Carell, for one, is now an Oscar-nominated movie star, and went on to act in hits like The Morning Show and Space Force. John Krasinski, meanwhile, has become a renowned director (hello, A Quiet Place) and action hero in Jack Ryan. As for fellow show alums Jenna Fischer and Angela Kinsey, they've continued to capitalize on the show, shifting their focus to podcasting, through their Office-themed podcast, Office Ladies.

There is no doubt that The Office has made the cast members millionaires, but just how much money is each actor from the show really worth, today? Whose net worth is the highest? Take a look below and see which cast members of the widely popular show earn the most money.

Updated November 2023: 'The Office' remains one of the most popular shows of all time. However, with the hit series leaving Netflix, fans of the series had to either watch the show syndicated on cable or subscribe to Peacock. But regardless of where 'The Office' can be seen, it is clear by the resumes of its stars that it launched the careers of just about all the actors. And with all the stars currently involved in television and filmmaking, it appears that their careers will continue to boost their net worths well into the future.

6 Paul Lieberstein

Net Worth: $14 Million

  • Character on The Office: Toby Flenderson

Fans might be shocked to learn that Paul Lieberstein, who played Toby Flenderson, Michael Scott's least favorite person, has surpassed many of his other cast mates with a net worth of $14 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Paul didn't only star on the show, however. He was also a writer and producer, so it makes sense why his earnings would surpass that of other cast members.

Paul has been a guest on the Office Ladies podcast and also joined the cast of Out of Office, which was created by the creators of The Office.

Additionally, Paul has been busy as a showrunner in 2023 for Lucky Hank. He also has a film, Not an Artist in post-production. A release date has yet to be given.
