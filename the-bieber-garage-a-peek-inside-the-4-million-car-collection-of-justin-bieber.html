From his humble beginnings on YouTube as a thirteen-year-old to performing at sold-out concert venues, Justin Bieber has been a pop sensation ever since manager Scooter Braun discovered him. At 27, the singer has amassed a $285 million net worth from his music sales, tours, and brand endorsements. Bieber loves to spend his money buying luxurious collectibles in every category. He has a stunning collection of watches, sneakers, and electronic gadgets. Adding to the list is his staggering collection of 15 cars, each more beautiful than the other. His enviable garage includes a combination of custom purchases and some invaluable gifts that he has preserved for over ten years and still uses them.

From European luxury automakers like Mercedes and Audi to US-based Cadillac and Fisker, the singer has cars from every spectrum. Let’s look at the cars Justin Bieber has in his garage.

Mercedes SLS AMG ($250,000)

A fan of German engineering, Justin Bieber owns a shiny white Mercedes SLS AMG that costs $250,000. The car gives an output of 571 HP and comes with a series of luxurious interior features. The singer is often spotted riding his SLS AMG with his wife, Hailey.

Lamborghini Aventador ($450,000)

Purchased for his 18th birthday, Justin Bieber has wrapped the Lamborghini Aventador more than once. The car was initially grey but later got a red wrap followed by a mint wrap with the words Cash Only spray-painted on the door, as noted by Motorious. The car is back to its original color, and Bieber purchased it for a $450,000 price tag.

Mercedes G65 AMG ($150,000)

The G-Wagon is one of Justin’s favorite cars. In 2017, he wrapped the car into a fun Christmas theme design. While the base price of the G65 AMG is $150,000, the customization added a few thousand more to the final bill.

Mercedes Maybach S600 ($400,000)

Another Mercedes in the collection, Justin Bieber bought himself a Maybach S600 that costs a whopping $400,000, one of the priciest cars in his collection.

Custom Hummer H2 ($90,000)

A combination of luxury and functionality, Bieber bought himself a Custom Hummer H2, a five-door car that provides comfort with off-road mobility. The vehicle costs $90,000.

Fisker Karma ($120,000)

A birthday gift from his manager Scooter Braun, the Fisker Karma is one of Bieber’s prized possessions. The $120,000 car was given to him on The Ellen Show as a surprise. As mentioned by Hot Cars, Bieber paid extra to apply the chrome wrap.

Cadillac CTS V Batmobile ($100,000)

Customized by the West Coast Customs, the Cadillac CTS V coupe was turned into a Batmobile for Bieber. The stunning coupe came with a black body kit, matching rims, and black tinted windows. The car cost him upwards of $100,000.

Bugatti Veyron Grand Sport ($2,000,000)

Bieber was gifted a $2 million Bugatti Veyron Grand Sport by music producer and rapper Birdman. The car was painted in glossy red and came with luxury features.

Custom Rolls Royce Wraith- ($330,000)

As stated by GQ, Justin Bieber purchased a Rolls Royce Wraith that was redesigned at West Coast Customs in 2018. The car was modified in height and came with silver wheel covers that move along with the wheel and make it look like it’s floating. The car cost him $330,000.

Audi R8 ($155,000)

Another West Coast Customs wrap occurred on his Audi R8 that lost its original dark color, and Bieber picked out a leopard print for the exterior. The car cost him $155,000.

Lamborghini Gallardo Spyder ($209,500)

A 16th birthday gift from rapper Puff Daddy, Justin Bieber, was given a $200,000 Lamborghini Gallardo Spyder as gift. Puff Daddy had promised him a car and stood true to his promise when he gifted one to Bieber.

Ferrari 458 ($239,340)

A marvelous piece of engineering, the Ferrari 458 can prance from 0 to 60 MPH in merely 3.4 seconds. Bieber purchased a blue 458 and installed a banger 2,000 W sound system in the $239,340 Ferrari.

Ferrari F430 ($86,166)

One of the first cars in his garage, Bieber purchased the Ferrari F430 when he was only 16 years old. According to Financial Express, the $86,166 car comes with a custom matte black finish and accelerates from 0 to 60 MPH in 4 seconds.

Lamborghini Huracan ($240,000)

One of his recent purchases is a bright orange $240,000 Lamborghini Huracan. He took social media to post a photo of himself with the fast supercar.

Mercedes-Benz Sprinter ($45,702)

Aside from his collection of fast cars, Justin Bieber also owns a Mercedes-Benz Sprinter that costs $45,702. It has a signature matte black finish and glossy black trim.

The other cars in his garage include Lamborghini Urus, Range Rover, and Porsche 911. Justin Bieber has a selective collection, and most of his automobiles are customized with West Coast Customs to give them a signature finish. No matter what street Justin Bieber drives on, his cars always stand out from the crowd.
