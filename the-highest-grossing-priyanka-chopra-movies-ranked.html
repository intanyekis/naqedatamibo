Priyanka Chopra is an Indian actress, singer, film producer, and philanthropist. She was born on July 18, 1982, in Jamshedpur, India. She won the Miss World pageant in 2000 and began acting in 2002 with the Tamil film Thamizhan. She later got into Bollywood films, where she starred in several films such as Aitraaz, Krrish, and Don. Priyanka became the first Indian woman to star in a television series on an American network.

In 2015, she debuted in American television with the series Quantico and has since starred in Hollywood films such as Baywatch and Isn't It Romantic. Priyanka has won several awards in her acting career and humanitarian work, including the Padma Shri, India's fourth-highest civilian award, and the UNICEF Goodwill Ambassadorship. Here are Priyanka Chopra's 8 highest-grossing movies.

Krrish 3 (2013) - $42.4 million

Krrish 3 is a Bollywood superhero film released in 2013. It is directed and produced by Rakesh Roshan, and it is the third installment in the krrish film series. The film features the lead roles of Priyanka Chopra, Hrithik Roshan, Vivek Oberoi, and Kangana Ranaut. It narrates the story of Krrish, a superhero with superhuman abilities, and battles the Kaal, who is on a mission to destroy the universe. Krrish 3 received mixed reviews from critics, with some praising the action sequences and performances, while others criticized the script and the lack of originality. However, the film was a commercial success, grossing $42.4 million worldwide.

Agneepath (2012) - $44.4 million

Agneepath is a Bollywood action drama directed by Karan Malhotra and produced by Karan Johar. It is a remake of the 1990 film of the same name. The film stars Hrithik Roshan in the lead role, with Priyanka Chopra, Sanjay Dutt, Rishi Kapoor, and Om Puri in supporting roles. The film was a commercial success and received positive reviews for Hrithik Roshan's performance and the film's action sequences. The film was a commercial success, grossing $44.4 million.

Bajirao Mastani (2015) - $46.2 million

Bajirao Mastani is a historical romance film that was released in 2015. The film is directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali and stars Ranveer Singh, Deepika Padukone, and Priyanka Chopra in lead roles. The film narrates the love story between Peshwa Bajirao, a Maratha warrior, and Mastani, a princess of Bundelkhand. The film portrays their love story against political tensions and power struggles in the 18th century. The movie's performances, soundtrack, and cinematography won praise from critics. Priyanka Chopra earned the Best Supporting Actress prize for her performance as Kashibai, Bajirao's first wife.

Don 2 (2011) - $49.3 million

Don 2 is a Bollywood action-thriller film released in 2011. It was directed by Farhan Akhtar and produced by Ritesh Sidhwani under the banner of Excel Entertainment. The film stars Shah Rukh Khan, Priyanka Chopra, Lara Dutta, and Boman Irani in lead roles for $49.3 million. The film received positive reviews from critics, was a commercial success, and grossed at the box office. It was also screened at the 62nd Berlin International Film Festival.

Planes (2013) -  $55.2 million

Planes are an animated film directed by Klay Hall and produced by DisneyToons. The movie is set in the same world as the previous film Cars, and this time they focus on airplanes. The story is about a small-town crop duster named Dusty Crophopper who dreams of becoming a racer despite his fear of heights. In this animated film, Planes, Priyanka Chopra voiced the character of Ishani, a sleek and confident Pan-Asian champion racer. The film grossed $55.2 million worldwide.

We Can Be Heroes II (2020) - $74 million.

We Can Be Heroes is a 2020 American superhero film written and directed by Robert Rodriguez. The film follows a group of superhero kids who unite to save their parents and the world from an alien invasion. The film features an ensemble of Hollywood actors YaYa Gosselin, Pedro Pascal, Priyanka Chopra, Christian Slater, and Boyd Holbrook. The film received positive reviews for its fun, entertaining storyline, and visual effects.

The Matrix Resurrections $159.2 million

The Matrix Resurrections is a science fiction action film directed by Lana Wachowski, who co-directed the original Matrix trilogy with her sister Lilly Wachowski. The film was released on December 22, 2021. The film is the fourth installment in The Matrix franchise, with Keanu Reeves returning to his role as Neo and Carrie-Anne Moss resuming her role as Trinity. The film also stars Yahya Abdul-Mateen II, Neil Patrick Harris, Jessica Henwick, Jonathan Groff, and Priyanka Chopra Jonas.

Baywatch (2017) - $177.9 million

Baywatch is a 2017 American action comedy film directed by Seth Gordon and based on the television series of the same name. The film stars Dwayne Johnson, Zac Efron, Alexandra Daddario, Kelly Rohrbach, and Priyanka Chopra. The film received mixed reviews from critics, with some praising the comedic performances of the cast while others criticizing it. Despite the mixed reviews, the film was a moderate box office success, grossing over $177 million worldwide against a budget of $69 million.
