About Honorary Consulate General of Austria in Houston, United States

Austrian General Honorary Consulate in Houston runs an inclusive range of consular services to local, Austrian, and international citizens in United-states.

The Austrian General Honorary Consulate in Houston supports American citizens through its consular services, if they:

  • Need general or specific info regarding Austrian economy, culture, sports, education and more
  • Need information regarding requirements and process of getting Austrian Citizenship
  • Specific contacts and information in Austria
  • Want to apply and obtain Austrian Visa

Visa and Passport services

Austrian passport and visa from are issued only to citizens whose main residence is in Austria and United-states.

The process of issuing Austrian Visa and Passport of Austria can take few weeks.

*Please contact with Austrian General Honorary Consulate in Houston if you have any question as regards of visa and passport matters.
