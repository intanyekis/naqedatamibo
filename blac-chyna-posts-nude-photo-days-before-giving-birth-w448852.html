Hot mama! Pregnant Blac Chyna posted a throwback nude photo of herself days before she is set to give birth to her baby girl with fiancé Rob Kardashian.

In the sizzling mirror selfie, a completely naked Chyna, 28, shows off her enviable curves and toned abs as she sits in bed, holding her arms over her breasts.

“Soon,” the Rob & Chyna star, who is clearly excited to snap back into shape after becoming a second-time mom, captioned the pic.

As previously reported, the Lashed Bar owner, who previously declared that she wanted to gain 100 pounds during her second pregnancy, most recently revealed her weight via Snapchat on October 11.

“4 weeks after Sunday she will be here !! Nov 16,” Chyna wrote alongside a photo of herself standing on a scale that read “186.6.”

“My goal is to gain, like, 100 pounds this pregnancy. Imma tear it up, then Imma snap back,” the 5-foot-2 E! personality told fans while snacking on chips during a Snapchat video this past June.

According to the National Institutes of Health, a woman of average height should gain only 25 to 35 pounds during her pregnancy. “Too much weight will increase the risk of pelvic floor disorders postpartum and later in life,” Dr. Rebecca Nelken, a Los Angeles–based Ob-Gyn, told Us Weekly earlier this year.

Months later, Chyna changed her mind and told her Snapchat followers that she didn’t want to gain any more weight during her pregnancy. She also revealed that she had adopted healthier habits.

“I started eating more clean and walking,” the expectant makeup artist shared. “From 135 to 183 to 176.2.”

Chyna — who is also mom of King Cairo, 4, with ex-fiancé Tyga — previously explained via social media that she plans to slim down postpregnancy by following a “strict” diet paired with waist training and working out.
