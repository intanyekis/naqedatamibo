Lance Stewart is a YouTube star who is popularly known for his vlogs and prank videos. He gained popularity through the Vine app earning more than 5 million followers on his Lance210 channel. His sister introduced Lance to vines and since then, he began working as a vineographer. He has collaborated with various other YouTubers for his videos with the most popular ones being with his former girlfriend Lizzy.

He started his YouTube channel on February 6, 2014, and within 4 months, it had more than 30k subscribers. Lance also does a lot of charitable work like buying burgers for homeless people. He makes a vlog every day at 4 pm and connects with his fans on a regular basis. Lance also has a website where he sells t-shirts and merchandise for extra income. His video ‘My Girlfriend Shows Her N*pples on Camera’ got more than 5 million views and became the most-watched video on his channel. Lance attracts a lot of people due to his fun-loving nature and that is what reflects in his work as well. He has an Instagram following of more than 6 million and his YouTube page has more than 6 million subscribers as well.
