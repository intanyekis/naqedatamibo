Who is Isabel Lucas?

Isabel Lucas is an award-winning Australian actress and model. Lucas is known for her acting in a movie like Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen and the TV series Home and Away.

Isabel Lucas: Age, Siblings, Parents, Ethnicity

Isabel Lucas was born on Jan 29, 1985, in Melbourne, Australia.

Her mother is Beatrice Lucas and her father is Andrew Lucas. Mrs. Beatrice is a teacher for special needs people and Mr. Andrew is a pilot.

She has one sibling, sister Nini Lucas.

She holds a mixed ethnicity of Swiss and Australian.

Education

Isabel Lucas initially studied at Jabiru Area School. She then joined St. Monica’s College, Cairns.

Furthermore, Isabel also learned drama from the Victorian College of Arts and  Queensland University of Technology.

Isabel Lucas: Professional Life, Career

Isabel was first sighted at the age of 12 for modeling in Melbourne. Then she developed her interest in acting and was approached by Sharron Meissner in June 2002.

Movies

Isabel was seen in several movies playing different roles. She was seen in the 2009 sci-fi movie Transformer: Revenge of the Fallen as Alice. In the same year, she appeared in Daybreakers, The Waiting City, and The Cove.

Her other featured movies are A Heartbeat Away (2011), Red Dawn (2012), That Sugar Film (2014), The Loft (2014), The 11th (2016), That’s Not Me (2017), In Like Flynn (2018), and more.

Her recent films of 2019 were In The Night and Brokers. In them, she played the characters of Marie and Tanya respectively.

TV Roles

Isabel’s popular role in the series Home and Away was very much appreciated. She was seen playing the role of Tasha Andrews from 2003-2006.

Her other series in a regular role as Samantha Cage was MacGyver. It is an American action-adventure show and Isabel was associated with it from 2017 to 2018.

Then, she was seen as Gwen and Anna in The pacific (2010) and Emerald City (2017).

Rumors and Controversy

Isabel Lucas was charged with interfering with international commerce by the Japanese police in 2007. She along with actress Hayden Panettiere, surfers David Rastovich and Vaya Phrachanh, paddled in order to save the Pilot wales from hunting in Taiji, Wakayama, Japan.

But they were halted by a fishing boat. To avoid arrest, they all left the country from Kansai International Airport. However, the warrant is still pending.
