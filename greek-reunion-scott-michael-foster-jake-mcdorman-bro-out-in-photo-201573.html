Boys’ night out! Greek stars Scott Michael Foster, Jake McDorman, Jacob Zachar, and Paul James came together for an epic reunion on Friday, Mar. 6, four years after the ABC Family series ended.

“The boys,” Foster, 30, captioned an Instagram photo. The longtime pals and former costars dressed casually for the outing while smiling big for the Kodak moment.

Foster played fan favorite Cappie in the series, which centered on fraternities and sororities at the fictional Cyprus-Rhodes University. As president of Kappa Tau Gamma, he often butt heads with his former best friend and president of Omega Chi Delta, Evan Chambers (McDorman). (Remember, don’t call him Bing!)

Foster went on to play Kristoff in the ABC series Once Upon a Time, and Leo in ABC Family’s Chasing Life. McDorman, 28, starred in the short-lived show Manhattan Love Story. He most recently starred opposite Bradley Cooper in the Oscar-nominated drama American Sniper.

Zachar, 28, played Rusty Cartwright in Greek. His lovable, nerdy freshman character often sought advice from Cappie and his sister, Zeta Beta Zeta president Casey Cartwright (Spencer Grammer). Zachar went on to guest star on TV shows such as House M.D. and CSI: Crime Scene Investigation.

James, meanwhile, played Calvin Owens. His character pledged with Omega Chi Delta, was Rusty’s best friend, and came out as gay during college. James, 32, has since appeared on Shameless, Grey’s Anatomy, and Torchwood.
