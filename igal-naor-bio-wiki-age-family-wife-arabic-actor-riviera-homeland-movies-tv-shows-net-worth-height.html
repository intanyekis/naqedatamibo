Igal Naor Biography And Wiki

Igal Naor, also known as Yigal Naor, is an Israeli actor. He has appeared in several American films such as Rendition and Munich, Green Zone. He also made his appearance on the famous Netflix series titled ‘Fauda’.

Igal Naor Education, Arabic

He studied at the Tel Aviv University in the theatre department. Unfortunately, he dropped out in his second year. He met Hasfari and Hanna Azoulay and together they began a theatre called The Simple Theatre.

It produced plays such as The Black Wedding Canopy (Huppah Shkhorah) and ‘Tashmad’. The plays enabled Naor to win acting awards at the 1982 and 1983 Acco Alternative Theater Festivals. Naor and his mates dispersed the group three years after its formation.
