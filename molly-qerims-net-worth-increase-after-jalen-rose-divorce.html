Why Did Molly Qerim And Jalen Rose Get Divorced?

Jalen Rose and Molly Qerim are two well-known faces you may recognize from ESPN. While they are normally in the headlines for their opinions on sports, the fact that they recently divorced has garnered a lot of attention from the media.

Although the divorce was amicable, the reasons for it are yet unknown. It has been theorized that Molly Qerim's friendship with Stephen A. Smith, a coworker, was the cause of Jalen Rose and Qerim's breakup. The rumor has caused speculations that the NBA players are beefing over Qerim’s attention.

Jalen Rose sets the record straight in his interview with GQ's Tyler R. Tynes, shutting down rumors that his split with Molly Qerim was due to her alleged relationship with Stephen A. Smith. Though Smith has angered a lot of people, Rose certainly isn’t one of them.

Rose confidently stated that he knew the rumors weren't true. He chose not to respond to them, as he believed it would only make those who spread them appear less credible.

“It’s not true. But, when it happened, it’s two ways for me to handle it as a professional: I work with him, I got love for him, and that's my brother. I got love for her, we were friends before we were married, we just didn’t work as a couple. I just saw her today. We cool! But for me to see the speculation and for people to assume that was why, it bothered me.”

Why the rumors "bothered" Jalen Rose is reasonable. It's completely understandable to feel uneasy about false information being spread, especially when it comes to such a sensitive topic.

Qerim cried in June 2021, just two months after Rose filed for divorce, as she talked about his difficulties and the deaths of several members of his family.

With tears streaming down her face, she lovingly referred to him as her 'husband' while sharing the heartbreaking news of losing five family members this year. As she spoke out about him, Molly referred to it as "a hard couple of years."

Rose is taking a stand in his divorce documents by refusing to ask for alimony and making it clear that he doesn't want Qerim to receive it either, according to the outlet. Qerim is not seeking spousal support, and there's no prenup in place.

Maybe the rumors about his divorce will finally die down now that Rose has cleared the air. The public nature of famous people's social lives frequently provides fertile ground for gossip.
