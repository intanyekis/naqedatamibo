Australian actor Liam Hemsworth has an estimated net worth of $26 million.

Hemsworth is best known for his breakout screen role as Gale, the childhood friend of Jennifer Lawrence's Katniss Everdeen in 2012's The Hunger Games. Liam started his acting career in Australian television, appearing as a recurring character on Neighbours in 2007 and 2008, and The Elephant Princess in 2008 and 2009.

His brother Chris snagged the role of Thor, and Liam landed roles in the thriller Triangle in 2009 and the romance The Last Song in 2010, alongside Miley Cyrus.

He has been praised as an up-and-coming film star and started dating former Disney queen Miley Cyrus. Liam and Miley Cyrus became engaged in June 2012 but split up the following year. The couple eventually got married on December 2018.

Hemsworth was born on January 13, 1990, in Melbourne, Australia.
