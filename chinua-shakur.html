Know 2pac Step-brother Chinua Shakur &amp; His Enigmatic Life

Chinua Shakur is well-recognized as the step-brother of American rapper, Tupac Shakur. The late rapper is considered one of the most influential and successful rappers of all time.

Despite being a celebrity brother, Shakur has been able to maintain a low profile and away from media reach. What does Tupac's step-brother do for a living? Where is he now? Let's find out as we explore the life of Chinua.

Chinua Shakur Relationship Status - Is Tupac Shakur Step-brother Dating Someone?

Chinua Shakur's dating life is out of media reach similar to Nicolo Robert Tucci. He maintains a relatively low profile and has not mentioned having a girlfriend or being married.

Shakur might be in a committed relationship with his partner and has decided to keep it only in a close-knit circle. Speaking of relationships, his famous brother Tupac Shakur was once married to Keisha Morris.

However, the marriage was annulled after ten months. The American rapper was also romantically linked with Jara Everett, Kidada Jones, Madonna, and so on.

Chinua Shakur Bio: Parents, Age & Early Life

Chinua Shakur was born from the union of Mutulu Shakur and Makini Shakur. His parents split after some time of togetherness. Like Tupac, his step-brother is not open to the public and most of his early life details including birthdate are out of the public eye.

Chinua is older than the All Eyez on Me singer who was born on June 16, 1971. His father, Mutulu, married Afeni Shakur in 1975 who played the role of a motherly figure in his life.

Chinua Shakur's Father, Mutulu Shakur, Was Politically Active

Mutula Shakur was politically active since his teen days. He was a member of the Black Liberation Army. In addition to his political works, Mutulu was a licensed and certified acupunterer.

Chinua's father founded and directed the Black Acupuncture Advisory Association of North America and the Harlem Institute of Acupuncture. Sadly, Mutulu died at the age of 72 on July 7, 2023, after being diagnosed with bone marrow cancer.

Look At Chinua Shakur Net Worth

While Tupac Shakur had an estimated net worth of $40 Million at the time of death, what is his step-brother, Chinua Shakur's net worth?  Well, Chinua has kept his professional life a secret just like his love life. 

What Chinua does for a living is a mystery. Unlike Chinua, Tupac's other siblings are millionaires. Sekyiwa Shakur has an estimated net worth of $40 Million as she is the CEO of the Tupac Amaru Shakur Foundation.

Chinua Shakur's Siblings & Step-Siblings

Chinua Shakur has five brothers and sisters. Among his siblings, Tupac Shakur had reached the height of popularity. Besides All Eyez on Me singer, his other step-siblings are Nzingha Shakur, Takerra Allen, Mopreme Shakur, Ayize Shakur, N’Neka. 

They are all grown-ups and doing well in their life. Unlike Chinua, his step-siblings constantly appear in public. Mopreme Shakur is a rapper like Tupac.

Similarly, Takkera Allen who is the youngest of all is an author. She has over 17 books published under her name.

Chinua Shakur's Father Was Arrested For Robbery

Mutulu Shakur who was a member of the Black Liberation Army was arrested for Robbery and murder. He was finally arrested by the FBI in California on February 12, 1986, after escaping capture for almost six years. 

Shakur was sentenced to 60 years in prison for his involvement in the Brink robbery where two policemen and a guard died. He sought reduced sentencing in 2019 but was denied. 

Later, Chinua's father, Mutulu, was granted parole on November 10, 2022, due to deteriorating health. Shakur was released on December 16, 2022, after serving 38 years. However, he died after 8 months of parole.

Chinua Shakur's Step-Sister, Sekyiwa Shakur, Legal Battles

Sekyiwa Shakur has been in some legal battles. She filed a lawsuit against the executor of their mother's estate, Tom Whalley in January 2022. According to Sekyiwa, Whalley has already paid himself over $5 Million from Amaru Foundation.

Similarly, Sekyiwa has also been involved in another legal battle in September 2019. She has claimed ownership of Tupac's album cover art and NFTs.
