How much is Dany Garcia's net worth?

CelebrityNetWorth estimates that Garcia's net worth is about $40 million. This is thanks to estimates based on the many films she has executive produced, her ownership stake in the XFL, and other entrepreneurial ventures.

How did Dany Garcia make her money?

Garcia has held more than one lucrative role in her decades-long career. She had an early career as an executive vice president at Merrill Lynch and also started her own wealth management firm. Around 2008 and the time of her divorce from Johnson, the two went into business together, with Garcia managing his career and eventually producing movies with him in the lead.
