Who is Olivia Macklin?

Olivia Macklin is an actress of American nationality. Olivia Macklin is primarily known for her work in projects like The Young Pope (2016), Radium Girls (2018), Dead to Me (2019), etc.

Recently in 2021, she did 10 episodes of Pretty Smart (TV Series) where she played the role of Claire. As herself, she appeared in Noches con Platanito (TV Series) and #NOTHONDURAS: Spring Break! (Video short).
